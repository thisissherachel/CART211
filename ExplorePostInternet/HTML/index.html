Here we explore Artie Vierkant’s piece on The Image Object Post-Internet
where they discuss the concept of Post-Internet art.

Post-Internet Art as described by Vierkant for the context of their piece is “a result of the contemporary moment: inherently informed by ubiquitous authorship, the development of attention as currency, the collapse of physical space in networked culture, and the infinite reproducibility and mutability of digital materials.”

Be exposed to a few quotes taken from Vierkant’s piece, ponder them,
then dive into the Post-Internet art discussion.
